From a grandmother's secret recipe to your favorite neighborhood café

Coffee Tales began in 1995 when Maria Santos turned her grandmother's old bookshop into a cozy coffee house. Armed with her abuela's handwritten recipe for the perfect Cuban espresso and a dream to create a gathering place for the community, she transformed the dusty shelves into warm oak tables.

Today, we still brew every cup with the same passion and precision that Maria brought to that first pot of coffee nearly three decades ago.

Maria Santos

We believe coffee is more than a beverage—it's a catalyst for connection, creativity, and community. Every bean we source tells a story of sustainable farming and fair trade partnerships.

The Coffee Journey

From Farm to Your Cup

Experience the meticulous process that makes every sip of Coffee Tales extraordinary. We believe great coffee starts long before the brewing begins.

Ethical Sourcing

We partner directly with small-batch farmers in Colombia and Ethiopia, ensuring fair wages and sustainable agricultural practices that protect the environment.

Artisan Roasting

Our master roasters carefully monitor temperature and time, roasting in small batches daily to unlock the unique flavor profile and aroma of each bean origin.

Precision Brewing

Whether it's a pour-over, espresso, or cold brew, our trained baristas use precise measurements and filtered water to extract the perfect cup every single time.
